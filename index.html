About Me

I’m Abhishek Paudel from Kapilvastu, Nepal. I am currently a Bachelor’s 2nd-year student pursuing BSc.IT at ISMT College, Yogikuti, Butwal. I completed my +2 in Computer Engineering from Shree Mahendra Mavi Secondary School. Along with my studies, I actively work in Graphics Design, Photography, and Content Creation. I am a passionate creator who puts time, effort, and creativity into every project I take. I love capturing real moments, designing clean and meaningful visuals, and creating content that reflects originality and uniqueness. I believe that my designs and photos should speak more than words.I continuously explore new skills and ideas, and I always aim to deliver the best version of my work. For me, creativity is not just a routine. it’s my lifestyle.
